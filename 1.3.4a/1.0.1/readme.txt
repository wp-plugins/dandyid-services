DandyID provides one place to manage you and your relationships online.

== Description ==

DandyID is a free service that connects all of your online identities and makes them globally accessible. Your e-mail address serves as a single access point to your Flickr, Twitter, LinkedIn, and much more -- services being added daily!

The DandyID Services Wordpress plugin dynamically accesses your DandyID services, and displays them in a sidebar widget.

== Installation ==

1. If you are upgrading from a previous version:
   a. Deactivate the DandyID Services plugin.
   b. Delete the DandyID Services plugin folder from your server.

2. Upload the DandyID Services plugin folder to /wp-content/plugins/ 

3. Login to your WP admin web page.

4. Activate the DandyID Services plugin:
   a. Click on the "Plugins" tab.
   b. Find DandyID Services in the list of Inactive Plugins (or Recently Active Plugins).
   c. Click on "Activate" to activate the DandyID Services plugin.

4. Configure the DandyID Services plugin:
   a. Click on the "Settings" tab.
   b. Click on the "DandyID Services" subtab.
   c. Enter your DandyID Service Options -- all fields are required:
      - Email Address ... The email address used as your login for DandyID.
      - Password ........ The password used as your login for DandyID.
      - User ID ......... Your DandyID user ID (e.g. 105).
      - Sidebar Title ... A title that will appear on your blog sidebar above your DandyID Services.
   d. Press the "Save" button to save your DandyID Service Options.

5. Setup the DandyID Services plugin to appear on your sidebar:
   a. Click on the "Design" tab.
   b. Click on the "Widgets" subtab.
   c. On the left side, next to DandyID Services, click on "Add" to make it appear in the list of "Current Widgets".
   d. Click on "Save Changes".

6. The plugin is now ready, and your DandyID Services should appear in your sidebar.
